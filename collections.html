Museum explanations

Koleksi Kerajaan

The museum is best known for preserving royal objects from the Gowa tradition. These explanations can later be expanded with photos, audio, or a real 360-degree room scan.

Royal Palace Building

The museum building represents a palace environment where hierarchy, hospitality, and ceremonial space meet in one timber structure.

Salokoa Crown

The Salokoa is described as a gold crown and one of the most important symbols of Gowa royal authority, especially in coronation traditions.

Keris and Royal Weapons

Keris, swords, and ceremonial weapons help visitors understand power, protection, diplomacy, and identity in the kingdom's material culture.

Jewelry and Regalia

Gold jewelry, bracelets, chains, and other regalia show how status, craft, belief, and court ceremony were expressed through objects.
